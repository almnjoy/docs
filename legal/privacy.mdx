## Summary

We take your privacy seriously. Here's the short version:

- Your private AI instance runs in its own isolated container. Nothing is shared across users.
- Your conversations and files are **not** used to train any AI model.
- We use upstream model providers (Anthropic, OpenAI, OpenRouter) to power AI responses — they have their own policies linked below.
- We store only what we need to operate the service.
- You can export or delete your data at any time.

---

## What we collect

**Information you give us:** email address, name, billing information (processed by our payment processor — not stored by us), your AI's name, conversations, uploaded files, workspace documents, and optionally API keys for BYOK plans (stored encrypted).

**Information we collect automatically:** log data (IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps) for security; basic usage metrics (token consumption, feature usage) for billing and capacity planning; device and session information.

**Cookies:** we use essential cookies for login sessions, plus optional analytics cookies. You can opt out of non-essential cookies in our cookie preferences.

---

## How we use your information

- To operate the service (run your AI instance, authenticate you, send transactional emails)
- To process payments and manage your subscription
- To enforce token usage caps and prevent abuse
- To respond to support requests
- To improve the service (aggregate, non-personal usage trends only)
- To comply with legal obligations

We do **not** sell your personal data, use your conversations or files to train AI models, or share your content with any third party except as strictly necessary to operate the service.

---

## Upstream model providers

To generate AI responses, prompts are sent to upstream model providers via OpenRouter, which routes to providers including OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, and others. Each provider has its own privacy policy. We use API tiers which by default do not retain inputs or outputs for training.

BYOK users communicate directly with their chosen provider using their own API key.

---

## Data storage and security

- Your AI instance runs in an isolated Docker container
- Instance data is encrypted at rest
- All communications use TLS (HTTPS)
- BYOK API keys are encrypted before storage
- Access controls and audit logging limit who can access infrastructure

---

## Data retention

- **Active account:** data retained while your account is active
- **Cancelled account:** data deleted within 30 days of cancellation
- **Billing records:** retained 7 years per US law
- **Logs:** retained 90 days for security purposes

## Your rights

You may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your data. To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@madeformeai.com. We will respond within 30 days.

**California residents:** Rights under CCPA apply, including the right to know what we collect and to opt out of any sale (we don't sell).

**EU/UK residents:** We comply with GDPR where applicable.
