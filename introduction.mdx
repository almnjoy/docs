## What makes it different

**It remembers you across every conversation.** Tell it your business idea on Monday and it still knows on Friday. No re-explaining yourself, no copy-pasting your whole life story into a chat box every time you open it.

**It's completely private.** Your conversations, your files, your connected apps — none of it is shared, none of it trains any AI model, none of it is read by us or anyone else. It's locked to you.

**It actually does things, not just answers questions.** Drafts an email for you. Builds a week-by-week plan for your business launch. Searches the web and gives you a real summary. Helps you think through a decision. It acts — it doesn't just talk.

**You don't need to know anything about AI to use it.** No prompts to memorize. No settings to configure. No jargon. If you can send a text message, you can use this. That's the whole point.

## Your own private link

When you sign up, you get your own address — something like `yourname.madeformeai.com`. That's your assistant. Nobody else can reach it. Nobody else sees what you talk about. It's yours.

You can bookmark it, add it to your phone's home screen, and use it exactly like an app.
