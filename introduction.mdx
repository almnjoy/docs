MadeForMeAI gives you a personal AI assistant that's set up just for you.

Unlike ChatGPT, Claude, or any shared AI tool — yours isn't shared with millions of other people. It has its own private space, its own memory, and it's only accessible to you.

## What makes it different

**It remembers you.** Tell it something on Monday and it still knows on Friday. No copy-pasting the same context every single session.

**It's only yours.** Your conversations, your files, your data — none of it is shared, none of it trains any AI model, none of it is read by us.

**It works with your stuff.** Connect WhatsApp, Gmail, Google Calendar, or your files. It fits into how you already work.

**It actually does things.** Drafts emails. Plans your week. Organizes files. Searches the web. Way more than just answering questions.

## Your own URL

When you sign up, you get your own private address — like `mark.madeformeai.com`. That's your assistant. Nobody else uses it or can access it.
