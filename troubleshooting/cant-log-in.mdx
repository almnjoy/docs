title: Can't Log In
description: Troubleshooting login issues.
---

## Check your token

Make sure you're pasting the full token from your welcome email. It's a long string — easy to accidentally cut off the end.

The token looks like this:
```
example-login-token-1234567890
```

Copy it directly from the email, don't retype it.

## Make sure you're at the right URL

Your URL is `yourname.madeformeai.com` — the name you chose during signup. Check your welcome email for the exact address.

## Try a different browser

If the page isn't loading or the login screen isn't appearing, try Chrome or Firefox. Clear your browser cache if problems persist.

## Token not working

If your token is definitely correct but still not working:

1. Check that you're on the right URL (your specific subdomain, not just madeformeai.com)
2. Try an incognito/private browser window
3. Reach out in [Discord](https://discord.gg/uEugVKA8PH) or email support@madeformeai.com — include your username/subdomain and we'll sort it out

## Lost your token

If you've lost your token and can't get in, email support@madeformeai.com from the email address you used to sign up. We'll verify your identity and get you back in.
