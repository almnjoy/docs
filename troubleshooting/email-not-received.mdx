---
title: Welcome Email Not Received
description: What to do if your welcome email didn't arrive.
---

## Check your spam folder

The most common cause. Search your spam/junk folder for an email from `your-ai@madeformeai.com`.

## Wait a few minutes

Your AI takes up to 2 minutes to provision after checkout. The email sends once it's ready. Give it a couple of minutes before worrying.

## Search your inbox

Search for `madeformeai` or `your-ai@madeformeai.com` across all folders including promotions, updates, and spam.

## Add us to your contacts

To prevent future emails going to spam, add `your-ai@madeformeai.com` to your contacts now.

## Still nothing after 5 minutes?

Reach out in [Discord](https://discord.gg/uEugVKA8PH) or email support@madeformeai.com with:
- The email address you used to sign up
- Roughly when you completed checkout

We'll look up your account and send your login details manually.
