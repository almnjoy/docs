title: Common Issues
description: Quick fixes for the most common problems.
---

## I didn't get my welcome email

Check your spam folder first. If it's not there:
- Wait 3-5 minutes — provisioning can take a moment
- Email support@madeformeai.com from the address you signed up with

Add `your-ai@madeformeai.com` to your contacts so future emails land in your inbox.

## I lost my login token

Email support@madeformeai.com from the address you used to sign up. We'll verify your identity and issue a new token.

## My AI seems slow or unresponsive

Try refreshing the page first. If it's still slow:
- Check [Discord](https://discord.gg/madeformeai) for any known outages
- Email support@madeformeai.com with your username (the name part of your URL)

## The WhatsApp QR code expired before I could scan it

Say "new QR" to the Setup Assistant and it will generate a fresh one immediately. QR codes expire in about 60 seconds.

## The Setup Assistant seems stuck or confused

Switch to a more capable model using the model selector (look for Claude Sonnet or GPT-4). Complex setup tasks like WhatsApp work better with stronger models.

## My AI forgot something it used to know

This can happen if memory was explicitly cleared, or if you're in a new conversation context. Just tell your AI directly:

> "Remember that I work in marketing and my main client is Acme Corp."

It will store it going forward.

## I want to access the full dashboard

If you signed up with the "Set it up for me" option, your default view is the clean chat interface. The full control panel is always available at:

`yourname-soul.madeformeai.com`

Replace `yourname` with your subdomain.

## Something else

Join [Discord](https://discord.gg/madeformeai) for the fastest response, or email support@madeformeai.com.
