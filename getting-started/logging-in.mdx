Your AI uses a **login token** instead of a password. This token was sent to you in your welcome email.

## How to log in

1. Go to your AI's URL (e.g. `mark.madeformeai.com`)
2. You'll see a prompt asking for your token
3. Copy the token from your welcome email and paste it in
4. Hit enter — you're in

## Finding your token

Your token is in the welcome email from `your-ai@madeformeai.com` with the subject line **"Your AI is ready"**.

It looks like a long string of random letters and numbers:
```
example-login-token-1234567890
```

<Warning>
Save this token somewhere safe — a password manager, a note, anywhere you won't lose it. We can't recover it for you if it's lost.
</Warning>

## Staying logged in

Once you log in, your browser remembers your session. You won't need to paste the token every time — only on new browsers or devices, or if you clear your browser data.

## Lost your token?

Reach out in [Discord](https://discord.gg/uEugVKA8PH) or email us at support@madeformeai.com and we'll get you back in.

## Logging in on a new device

Just paste your token again on the new device. Each device you log in on will be remembered separately.
