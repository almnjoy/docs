---
title: Your AI's URL
description: Understanding your private AI address.
---

When you sign up, you choose a name for your AI. That name becomes your private URL.

If you chose `mark`, your AI lives at:

```
mark.madeformeai.com
```

## What this means

- **Only you can access it.** The URL is private by default. Your login token is required to get in.
- **It's always on.** Your AI runs 24/7. You can come back any time and it picks up right where you left off.
- **Bookmark it.** Add `yourname.madeformeai.com` to your browser bookmarks for fast access.

## Can I change my URL?

Not currently. The name you picked during signup is permanent. Choose carefully.

If you really need to change it, reach out in [Discord](https://discord.gg/uEugVKA8PH) and we'll sort it out manually.

## Can I use a custom domain?

Custom domain support (e.g. `ai.yoursite.com`) is on the roadmap. Not available yet.
