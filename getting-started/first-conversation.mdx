Your AI is ready. Here's how to get the most out of it from the start.

## Just talk to it

No special commands. No prompts to memorize. Just type what you need like you're texting someone.

Some things to try right away:

- *"What can you help me with?"*
- *"Here's what I'm working on this week — help me prioritize."*
- *"Draft an email to my team about our meeting tomorrow."*
- *"Summarize this document for me"* — then paste in the text

## Tell it about yourself

The more your AI knows about you, the better it gets. Start by telling it who you are and what you do:

> *"I'm a freelance designer. I work with small business clients, usually on branding and websites. I'm pretty busy and need help staying organized."*

It will remember this. You won't need to explain yourself every session.

## It remembers everything you tell it

Unlike ChatGPT, your AI keeps memory across conversations. Anything you tell it stays in its memory until you ask it to forget.

This means:
- It gets smarter and more personalized over time
- You don't repeat yourself
- It can reference things you said weeks ago

[Learn more about memory →](/features/memory)

## Connect your tools

Once you're comfortable, connect Gmail or Google Calendar so your AI can actually do things — read emails, check your schedule, draft replies.
