## How to cancel

Go to the customer portal (link in your welcome email) and cancel from there. No forms, no calls, no runaround. One click.

Your subscription stays active until the end of your current billing period.

## What happens to your data

When you cancel:

1. **Your instance stays up** until the end of your billing period
2. **At the end of the period** your container is stopped and your URL goes offline
3. **Your data is held for 30 days** in case you come back
4. **After 30 days** all your data is permanently deleted — conversations, files, memory, everything

## Exporting your data

Want to keep your conversations or files before cancelling? You can download them from within your AI before your access ends. We're working on a formal export tool — for now, copy/paste anything important before you go.

## Coming back after cancellation

If you resubscribe within 30 days, reach out to us and we can restore your data. After 30 days it's gone permanently.

## Refunds

We don't offer refunds for partial months, but if something went wrong on our end reach out and we'll make it right. Email support@madeformeai.com or ping us in [Discord](https://discord.gg/uEugVKA8PH).
