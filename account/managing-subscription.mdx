---
title: Managing Your Subscription
description: Update billing, change payment method, or view your plan.
---

Your subscription is managed through LemonSqueezy, our payment processor.

## Access your billing portal

In your welcome email, there's a link to your **customer portal**. From there you can:

- View your current plan and billing date
- Update your payment method
- Download invoices
- Cancel your subscription

If you can't find the link, email us at support@madeformeai.com and we'll send it again.

## Upgrading your plan

Want to move from Standard to Power, or BYOK to Standard? Reach out in [Discord](https://discord.gg/uEugVKA8PH) or email support@madeformeai.com — we'll handle it manually while self-serve upgrades are being built.

## Changing your payment method

Go to the customer portal (link in your welcome email) → Update payment method.

## Billing date

You're billed on the same day each month as your original signup date.

## Failed payments

If a payment fails, LemonSqueezy will retry automatically. You'll get an email if there's an issue. If your subscription goes past-due your instance will remain active for a short grace period — update your payment method before it lapses.
