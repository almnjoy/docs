MadeForMeAI offers three plans. All plans include your own private AI instance, your own URL, and persistent memory.

## Plans

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="BYOK" icon="key">
    **$15/month**

    For users who already have their own API key from OpenAI or Anthropic. You pay the AI provider directly for usage — we run your private setup.

    Best for: developers and power users who already have API access.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Standard" icon="star">
    **$30/month**

    Everything included and ready to go. No API keys, no setup. Just sign up and start talking.

    Best for: most people.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Power" icon="bolt">
    **$60/month**

    For heavy daily use. Higher usage limits, faster responses, priority access to better models.

    Best for: people who rely on their AI all day, every day.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What's included in every plan

- Your own private URL (e.g. `yourname.madeformeai.com`)
- Persistent memory across all sessions
- Web search
- File workspace
- WhatsApp integration
- No data shared with other users
- No conversations used to train AI models
- Cancel anytime

---

## Which plan should I pick?

**Start with Standard** if you're not sure. It's the right fit for most people — everything included, no setup needed.

**Pick BYOK** if you already have an active OpenAI or Anthropic API account and want to keep using your own key at a lower monthly cost.

**Pick Power** if you're using your AI heavily throughout the day and hitting limits on Standard, or if you want consistently faster response times.

---

## Switching plans

Email support@madeformeai.com to switch tiers. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
