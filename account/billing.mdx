## Billing

Plans renew automatically each month on the anniversary of your signup. You'll receive a receipt by email after each charge.

Payments are processed securely by our payment processor. We never store your card details.

## 7-day money-back guarantee

If you're not happy in your first 7 days, email support@madeformeai.com and we'll refund you — no questions asked.

After the first 7 days, payments are non-refundable, but you can cancel at any time to stop future billing.

## Cancellation

Cancel anytime from your billing portal — no contracts, no runaround. Your access continues through the end of your paid period.

After cancellation:
- Your AI instance stays available until the end of your paid period
- Your data (conversations, files, memory) is held for 30 days
- If you resubscribe within 30 days, your data can be restored
- After 30 days, everything is permanently deleted

## Price changes

We may change prices at any time. Existing subscribers will be notified at least 30 days before any price increase affects them. You can cancel before the new price takes effect.
