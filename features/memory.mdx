title: Memory
description: Your AI remembers you — across every session.
---

Most AI tools forget you the moment you close the tab. MadeForMeAI is different.

## How it works

Your AI builds up a picture of you over time through your conversations. It remembers:

- Things you've told it directly ("I'm a freelance designer, my main client is Acme Corp")
- Your preferences ("I like short bullet-point summaries, not long paragraphs")
- Ongoing projects and context ("we've been working on the Q3 proposal")
- Your style and how you like things done

The more you use it, the better it gets. You don't need to re-introduce yourself every session.

## Telling it things directly

You can explicitly tell your AI to remember something:

> "Remember that I prefer to work in the mornings and my daughter's name is Lily."

It will store that and use it naturally in future conversations.

## What it doesn't remember

- Your AI does not share memory with anyone else's AI — it's completely isolated.
- Memory is tied to your instance. If you cancel and resubscribe after 30 days, memory starts fresh.

## Correcting or updating memory

Just tell it:

> "Actually, I changed jobs — I'm now at a different company."
> "Forget that I mentioned the Acme project, it's done."

Your AI will update its understanding accordingly.
