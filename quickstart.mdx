title: Quickstart
description: Get your AI up and running in under 2 minutes.
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your AI's name">
    Go to [madeformeai.com/early-access](https://madeformeai.com/early-access) and type the name you want for your AI's URL.

    For example, type `mark` and your AI will live at `mark.madeformeai.com`.

    <Note>Letters and numbers only, 3–20 characters. You can't change this later, so pick something you like.</Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pick a plan and check out">
    Select BYOK, Standard, or Power — then click **Get started**. You'll go through a quick checkout. No commitment, cancel anytime.

    Not sure which plan? Standard is the right choice for most people.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check your email">
    Within a minute or two you'll get an email from `your-ai@madeformeai.com` with your login token and your AI's URL.

    <Warning>Check your spam folder if you don't see it. Add `your-ai@madeformeai.com` to your contacts to make sure future emails land in your inbox.</Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log in with your token">
    Go to your AI's URL (e.g. `mark.madeformeai.com`). When prompted, paste the token from your welcome email.

    Save that token somewhere safe — it's the only way to log in.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start talking">
    That's it. Just say what you need. No setup, no tutorial, no learning curve. Your AI is ready.
  </Step>
</Steps>
