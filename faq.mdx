---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to know anything about AI to use this?">
    Nope. If you can text or send an email, you can use this. Just talk to it like a person. There's nothing technical to set up or learn.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is this the same as ChatGPT?">
    No — and that's the whole point. ChatGPT has hundreds of millions of users and forgets you the second you close the tab. MadeForMeAI is your own private assistant that remembers you, knows your context, and is set up around the way you work. Nobody else shares it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my stuff private?">
    Yes. Your instance is completely yours. Your conversations and files don't train any AI, don't get shared with anyone, and aren't read by us. It's locked to you.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does it learn about me?">
    By talking with you. The more you use it, the more it gets your style, your goals, and how you like things done. You can also tell it things directly — it will remember them.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I cancel anytime?">
    Yes. No contracts, no hidden fees, no runaround. Cancel anytime from your billing portal. Your data is held for 30 days after cancellation in case you come back, then permanently deleted.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to my data if I cancel?">
    Your data is held for 30 days after cancellation. If you resubscribe within 30 days we can restore it. After 30 days it's permanently deleted — conversations, files, memory, everything.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a money-back guarantee?">
    Yes. If you're not happy in your first 7 days, email support@madeformeai.com and we'll refund you — no questions asked.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is BYOK?">
    BYOK means Bring Your Own Key. If you already have an account with OpenAI or Anthropic, you can connect your own API key on the $15/month BYOK plan. You pay the AI provider directly for usage — we just run your private setup.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What can it actually do?">
    Draft emails and documents, schedule meetings, organize your files, search the web, plan your week, analyze documents you send it, take notes, connect to WhatsApp, help you think through decisions — basically whatever you'd ask a real assistant to do.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use my own domain?">
    Not yet. Custom domain support (e.g. ai.yourcompany.com) is on the roadmap. Right now your AI lives at yourname.madeformeai.com.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a mobile app?">
    Not yet — but the web interface works great on mobile browsers. Just bookmark your AI's URL on your phone's home screen.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I lose my login token?">
    Email support@madeformeai.com from the address you signed up with. We'll verify your identity and get you a new token.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I get help?">
    The fastest way is [Discord](https://discord.gg/madeformeai). You can also email support@madeformeai.com.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
